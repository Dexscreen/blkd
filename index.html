What is BlockDAG?

Why BlockDAG Technology Changes Everything

BlockDAG (Directed Acyclic Graph blockchain) is a Layer-1 network that replaces the traditional linear chain with a parallel block graph structure. Multiple blocks are confirmed simultaneously — eliminating bottlenecks and dramatically increasing throughput without sacrificing security.

Unlike Ethereum or Bitcoin, the BlockDAG network processes thousands of transactions per second natively. The BDAG crypto token powers every transaction, smart contract, and validator reward on the network.
